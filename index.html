Our mission

Fire Fighting AI Foundation exists to close the gap between what we know about fire risk and what we actually do about it. We use AI to turn scattered data into clear, actionable insight—so communities can act before a spark becomes a disaster.

  • Prevention first
  • Field-ready tools
  • Transparent, ethical AI
  • Community-centered design

Why we exist

Every year, fires destroy homes, habitats, and lives. Many of these incidents are predictable—and preventable. We believe technology should serve as an early shield, not just a late alarm.

Rising risk

Longer seasons, higher stakes

Hotter temperatures, longer fire seasons, and denser development are increasing fire danger across regions that were once considered low risk.

Fragmented data

Signals, but no single picture

Critical information is scattered across agencies, sensors, and reports—making it hard to see risk clearly or act in time.

Under-resourced

Communities left behind

High-risk areas often lack tools, training, and real-time insights. We focus on practical, accessible solutions that can be deployed where they’re needed most.

What we do

We design and deploy AI-powered tools that support prevention, planning, and response—always in partnership with the people on the front lines.

Risk intelligence

Community risk mapping

We develop AI models that analyze weather, vegetation, infrastructure, and historical incidents to identify high-risk zones and patterns.

  • Neighborhood-level risk scores
  • Scenario-based planning maps
  • Prioritized mitigation recommendations
Decision support

Tools for first responders

We design decision-support tools that help agencies simulate fire spread, assess resource needs, and visualize risk in real time.

  • Pre-incident planning dashboards
  • Dynamic resource allocation insights
  • Training and tabletop simulation support
Compliance & prevention

Clear guidance from complex codes

We translate complex codes, regulations, and best practices into clear, actionable guidance—powered by AI—to help property owners and communities stay safer and compliant.

  • Property-level risk and compliance checkups
  • Tailored mitigation checklists
  • Support for inspectors and HOAs
Education & open knowledge

Learning and collaboration

We create accessible resources, workshops, and open datasets so that students, researchers, and local leaders can build on and improve fire safety solutions.

  • Workshops and training sessions
  • Open, documented datasets where possible
  • Student and research collaborations

Our approach

Technology alone doesn’t solve safety. Our work is grounded in the lived experience of firefighters, inspectors, and residents.

Human-centered

We build with firefighters, inspectors, and residents—not just for them. Every tool is shaped by real-world workflows and constraints.

Transparent & ethical

We prioritize explainable models, clear documentation, and responsible use. Safety, equity, and accountability guide every deployment.

Collaborative

We partner with agencies, universities, and community organizations to test, refine, and scale what works—sharing lessons along the way.

Who we serve

Our work is designed to support everyone with a stake in fire safety—from the first unit on scene to the resident clearing brush in their backyard.

Fire departments & first responders
Seeking better situational awareness, planning tools, and training support rooted in real data.
Cities, counties & HOAs
Needing data-driven fire prevention strategies, risk mapping, and compliance support for their communities.
Residents & property owners
Wanting to understand and reduce their fire risk with clear, practical, and local guidance.
Researchers & students
Exploring applied AI for public safety and looking for real-world problems, data, and collaboration opportunities.
